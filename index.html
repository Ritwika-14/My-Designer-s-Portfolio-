A LITTLE ABOUT ME

I have a real passion for development and design that's been with me since long. I absolutely enjoy creating order from chaos, whether it's in organizing spaces, decluttering, or coming up with stylish decorations. These passions, combined have drawn me towards the field. As a college sophomore, I'm really drawn to the world of UI/UX Design. I've got a knack for spotting problems and finding innovative solutions , which I find incredibly satisfying. My passion for creativity and problem-solving is what fuels me, and I'm excited about my journey into the world of design , and I'm eager to discover where this path will lead me .
